About Me

Who I Am

Welcome! I’m Vorayote Kaewchit, a multidisciplinary designer, creator, and self-taught full-stack developer.

With a passion for merging creativity and technology, my work is driven by a commitment to bringing ideas to life through design and development.

Background

My journey in design began with formal studies in Fashion Design for 1 year, followed by 2 years in Textiles and Surface Design. Since then, I’ve been dedicated to expanding my skill set, teaching myself full-stack development, learning French, and continuously exploring new creative and technical avenues.

Hobbies & Interests

Outside of my professional pursuits, I enjoy DJing, music production, exercise, art, and cooking. I’m also passionate about travel, software engineering, UX/UI design, photography, drawing, learning, reading, and film acting. These interests not only fuel my creativity but also inspire the diverse perspectives I bring into my work.
